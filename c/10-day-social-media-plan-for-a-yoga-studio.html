Your 10-day plan, day by day

DayThemeWhat to post
Day 1 Introduce the offer Announce a new-member intro offer with a scroll-stopping image and a clear “here’s what you get.”
Day 2 Behind the scenes A candid look at what makes your yoga studio different — the people, the process, the details.
Day 3 Social proof Share a real review or a customer story that speaks to the people you want more of.
Day 4 Educate Answer the one question students looking for calm, community, and a consistent practice always ask before they commit.
Day 5 Reel A 15-second “Sunday reset” flow — three poses, calming voiceover, class times on the end card.
Day 6 Offer reminder Nudge on a new-member intro offer with a fresh angle and a deadline.
Day 7 Community Feature a regular, a staff pick, or a local partner — show you belong here.
Day 8 Reel Time-lapse of the studio filling up before a packed evening class, set to a lo-fi track.
Day 9 Last call Final reminder on a new-member intro offer — urgency + a single, obvious next step.
Day 10 Thank you + next Thank everyone who engaged and tease what’s coming next so momentum carries.
