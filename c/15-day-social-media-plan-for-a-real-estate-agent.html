A 15-Day Social Media Plan for a Real Estate Agent

The hardest part of social media for a real estate agent isn’t any single post — it’s keeping it going for two straight weeks. Here’s a proven 15-day arc built around a free home valuation or a new listing tour, tuned for buyers and sellers deciding whether now is the right time to move. Your 15-day plan, day by day

DayThemeWhat to post
Day 1 Introduce the offer Announce a free home valuation or a new listing tour with a scroll-stopping image and a clear “here’s what you get.”
Day 2 Behind the scenes A candid look at what makes your real estate agent different — the people, the process, the details.
Day 3 Social proof Share a real review or a customer story that speaks to the people you want more of.
Day 4 Educate Answer the one question buyers and sellers deciding whether now is the right time to move always ask before they commit.
Day 5 Reel A 20-second walkthrough of a new listing’s best three rooms, with the price reveal at the end.
Day 6 Offer reminder Nudge on a free home valuation or a new listing tour with a fresh angle and a deadline.
Day 7 Community Feature a regular, a staff pick, or a local partner — show you belong here.
Day 8 Value post A quick tip or list your audience will save and come back to.
Day 9 Reel “Three things buyers get wrong about this market” — quick talking-head with on-screen captions.
Day 10 Story-style A day-in-the-life sequence that makes people feel the vibe before they visit.
Day 11 Testimonial A second, different customer voice — variety builds trust.
Day 12 Objection-buster Tackle the reason someone hesitates about a real estate agent like yours, head-on.
Day 13 Reel Sold-in-a-weekend story: listing photo, offer count, and the happy handover.
Day 14 Last call Final reminder on a free home valuation or a new listing tour — urgency + a single, obvious next step.
Day 15 Thank you + next Thank everyone who engaged and tease what’s coming next so momentum carries.
